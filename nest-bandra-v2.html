Our Story

Not just a café.
It's a nest.

Tucked on the first floor of Hotel Metro Palace, above the buzz of Hill Road, The Nest is Bandra's favourite hideout for slow mornings and long evenings — string lights, potted plants, board games, and a menu that moves from Turkish eggs to truffle risotto without missing a beat.

Whether it's a cold coffee before work, a pasta lunch with friends, or shisha and mocktails past midnight, this rooftop nest holds space for all of it.
